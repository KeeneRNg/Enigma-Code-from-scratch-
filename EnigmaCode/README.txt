Step 1: After executing the code, choose whether you want to encrypt or decrypt (The overall algorithm is the same both ways, it just changes whether it says it is a ciphertext or plaintext at the end

Step 2: Enter 10 letter pairs separated by commas, letters should not be paired to themselves

Step 3: Enter the starting position of the rotors, separated by commas. The order is the first rotor starting position as the right most number, and the third rotor as the left most number

Step 4: Enter the text to be encrypted/decrypted

Step 5: Output will be shown on screen
